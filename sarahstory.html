Chapter 1: Inspiration

This skirt was inspired by old Hollywood glam and a Viktor and Rolf collection of couture tulle skirts and dresses. The color palette is pulled from the fall of 2013 Pantone color story.

Chapter 2: Original Sketches

Creating a map of steps for the construction of the skirt and sketching the mini pattern pieces allows me to visualize the best way to put the garment together. This helps to minimize fabric waste and reduce mistakes made while sewing.

Chapter 3: Muslin Draping

Three stages of draping tulle layers. This muslin is used to create the patterns.
